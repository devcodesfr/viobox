# AI Usage

AI assistance may be used while building Viobox for debugging, documentation, code review, and analysis suggestions. It may also be used to suggest possible quality issues from transcripts.

AI will not be treated as the final source of truth for bug reports. Final conversation behavior, findings, severity, and submission notes will be manually verified before delivery. The voice bot should rely on scripted scenario facts first, with any LLM assistance limited to safer wording and never allowed to invent patient details.
